MATE ROV Project

Background

The MATE-ROV Projetc is a project hosted by MATE international. It is a competition to teach science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) and prepare students for technical careers. Wentworth IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is working on a ROV to compete in the June International competition. I was named the mechanical lead for this project, which meant that I was able to work in collaboration with my peers in order to develop all the mechanical designs for the ROV. This taught me valuable lessons in regards to how to follow the design process, design for manufacturability, and is currently pushing my manufacturing ability to new levels
